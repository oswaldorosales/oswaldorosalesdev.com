# OG Image Template

Create an Open Graph image for social media sharing.

## Requirements

- **Size:** 1200x630 pixels
- **Format:** PNG or JPG
- **Filename:** `og-image.png`
- **Location:** `public/og-image.png`

## Content Suggestions

Include:
- Your name in large text
- Your title/role
- Simple background (solid color or gradient)
- Optional: Your photo or logo

## Design Tools

- [Canva](https://www.canva.com) - OG Image template
- [Figma](https://www.figma.com) - Custom design
- [og-image.vercel.app](https://og-image.vercel.app) - Automated generation

## Quick Option

Use your profile photo with text overlay:
1. Open profile.avif in photo editor
2. Add text: "Oswaldo Rosales | Software Engineer"
3. Export as 1200x630 PNG
4. Save as `public/og-image.png`
